Planned server features

Here is the kind of world we want to build: something playful, a bit nerdy, recognizably medical, and easy to join for both hardcore players and people who just want a peaceful evening on campus vibes in block form.

🏥 Hospital Spawn

A central spawn inspired by a blocky teaching hospital with emergency wing, ward signs, clinic corridors, and pharmacy counters.

🧪 Pharmacy Lab

Brewing-heavy spaces themed like pharmaceutics labs, recipe rooms, herb gardens, and potion stations with academic flavor.

🦴 Anatomy Zone

Large-scale bones, organs, anatomy halls, museum-style builds, and themed parkour or discovery areas for fun exploration.

🌙 Low-stress SMP

Friendly survival focus, clean rules, community building, and a better place to unwind after long days of lectures or exams.

🎓 FMPT Identity

Student groups, promo banners, faculty references, med/pharma humor, and world landmarks that actually feel ours.

🛡️ Useful Protections

Depending on the final setup: anti-grief, moderation tools, secure spawn area, and quality-of-life rules to keep it chill.
